About Us

RedJarvians Cricket Club specializes in organizing Under 12 and Under 14 cricket matches, tournaments, and weekend cricket events across Bangalore. We provide young cricketers with opportunities to gain match experience, improve their skills, and compete in a professional and enjoyable environment. Our focus is on player development, sportsmanship, teamwork, and creating memorable cricket experiences for aspiring young talent.
